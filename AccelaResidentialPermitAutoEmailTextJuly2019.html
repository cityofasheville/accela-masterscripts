I. PERMIT APPROVED/ISSUED

Email Subject: Permit Approved

Permit Number: xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Location: xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Your residential permit application has been approved. For your convenience, you may visit the Citizen Access website (https://services.ashevillenc.gov/citizenaccess) to print your permit and approved plans/comments.

Please note that the issued permit along with the approved plans/comments must be maintained in hard copy on the project site during construction until the permit is closed.

Please refer to the following steps to access your approved permit and plans/comments online in .PDF format:

1. Visit https://services.ashevillenc.gov/citizenaccess. Register for a Citizen Access account if you have not already done so, then log in to access the permit documents.  

2. Enter your project’s permit number in the top right search box and click on the green spyglass to pull up the permit record.

3. Click Record Info to access a drop-down menu; then select Attachments from the drop-down menu.

4. To download the 1) issued permit and 2) approved plans/comments, click the blue links next to documents labeled ISSUED PERMIT and APPROVED SITE PLANS + COMMENTS and/or APPROVED BUILDING PLANS + COMMENTS.

If you have questions, please contact the Permit Application Center at PAC@ashevillenc.gov or 828-259-5846 or visit in-person at 161 S. Charlotte St. on Monday-Friday from 8:30 am - 5:00 pm.

II. PERMIT ON HOLD

Email Subject: Action Needed On Your Permit

Permit Number: xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Location: xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Your residential permit application has been reviewed and requires additional information and/or revision. For your convenience, you may visit the Citizen Access website (https://services.ashevillenc.gov/citizenaccess) to download reviewed plans and comments.

Please refer to the following steps to download reviewed plans and comments online in .PDF format:

1. Visit https://services.ashevillenc.gov/citizenaccess. Register for a Citizen Access account if you have not already done so, then log in to access the permit documents.  

2. Enter your project’s permit number in the top right search box and click on the green spyglass to pull up the record.

3. Click Record Info to access a drop-down menu; then select Attachments from the drop-down menu.

4. To download reviewed plans and comments, click the blue links next to documents labeled REVIEWED SITE PLANS + COMMENTS and/or REVIEWED BUILDING PLANS + COMMENTS.

You may resubmit your revised plans and response to comments online in .PDF format through develop.ashevillenc.gov or in person during business hours at 161 S. Charlotte St.

For additional information and resubmittal guidelines, visit http://bit.ly/dsd_new_residential.

Please note that your plans may be reviewed by staff from multiple divisions, including the Building Safety Division (“Building”), Planning and Zoning Division (“Planning”), and Site Engineering Division (“Site”) depending on your scope of work. You will receive additional notification emails if your application receives plan review comments from other divisions.

If you have questions, please contact the Permit Application Center at PAC@ashevillenc.gov or 828-259-5846 or visit in-person at 161 S. Charlotte St. on Monday-Friday from 8:30 am - 5:00 pm.
